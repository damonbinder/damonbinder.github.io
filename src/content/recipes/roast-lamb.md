---
title: Roast Lamb
source: Apicius 8.6.4
category: Meat
subcategory: Lamb and Goat
order: 48
---

**Ingredients**

* 2 kg leg of lamb  
* 250 ml fish sauce  
* 750 ml water  
* 100 ml olive oil  
* 1 tsp asafoetida  
* 1 tsp pepper

**Method**

1. Place the leg of lamb in a large pot or dutch oven along with the fish sauce, olive oil, and water. Gently simmer for around 1 hr, or until the center of the lamb reaches 65C.  
2. Preheat the oven to 260C/500F.   
3. Place the leg of lamb on a roasting tin and score the fat. Mix the asafoetida and pepper together with a little of the cooking liquid, and use it to coat the leg of lamb.   
4. Place in the oven and cook until the lamb is golden. Remove from the oven, allow to rest for a few minutes, and then carve and serve along with a sauce.
