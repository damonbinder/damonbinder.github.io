---
title: Fava Beans
source: Apicius 5.6.3
originalLang: Latin
original: >-
  aliter: fabaciae ex sinapi trito melle nucleis ruta cumino; ex aceto
  inferuntur.
translation: >-
  Another way: fava beans with ground mustard, honey, pine nuts, rue, and
  cumin; they are served up with vinegar.
category: Legumes
subcategory: Other Legumes
order: 155
---

**Ingredients**

* 400 g cooked, peeled fava beans  
* 50 g pine nuts  
* 2 Tb Djon mustard  
* 1 Tb honey  
* 1 Tb vinegar  
* ½  tsp cumin  
* ½ tsp rue

**Method**  
Finely crush the pine nuts and mix with Djon mustard, honey, vinegar, cumin, and rue. Toss with the fava beans and serve.
