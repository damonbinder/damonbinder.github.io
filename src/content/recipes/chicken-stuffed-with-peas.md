**Ingredients**

* 1.5 kg whole chicken  
* 150 g split peas  
* 150 g cooked *lucanicea* or sweet Italian sausage, finely chopped  
* 3 eggs  
* ½ tsp pepper  
* ½ tsp lovage  
* 1 tsp oregano  
* ½ ts ginger  
* 60 ml fish sauce  
* 120 ml *passum*  
* 120 ml white wine  
* 450 g streaky bacon

**Method**  
*Making the sauce*

1. Place into a small saucepan the pepper, lovage, oregano, ginger, fish sauce, *passum*, and white wine. Bring to a gentle simmer and cooked for a few minutes, then remove from the heat.

*Making the stuffing*

1. Place 450 ml of water into a saucepan and bring to the boil. Add the split peas, cover, and cook until the peas are soft and starting to go mushy. Remove and drain any excess water.  
2. Whisk together the eggs, then gently cook in a frying pan until the have just begun to set.  
3. Mix together peas, sausage, and eggs, then flavor with a third of the sauce.

*Creating the final dish*

1. Preheat the oven to 200C/400F.  
2. Galantine the chicken, then stuff with the stuffing and tie off with butcher’s twine  
3. Wrap the chicken with the bacon.  
4. Place the chicken in the oven, and cook for about X minutes  
5. Once the chicken is cooked, remove from the oven and allow to rest for several minutes before slicing. Serve each slice with a little more of the prepared sauce.
