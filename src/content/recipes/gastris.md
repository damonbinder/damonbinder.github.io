**Ingredients**

* 200g sesame seeds  
* 200g honey  
* 100g almonds  
* 100g hazelnuts  
* 100g walnut  
* 50g poppyseed  
* 1 tsp ground black pepper  
* Oil, for greasing

**Method**

1. Place the sesame seeds into a food processor and blend into a powder.  
2. Put 80g of the honey into a small saucepan. Bring to a boil and simmer for 6 minutes. Remove from the heat, add the sesame seed mixture, and stir together.  
3. Once the sesame seed mixture is cool enough to touch, turn out onto a greased benchtop and knead together. Divide the mixture in two and roll out into two identical thin sheets using a greased rolling pin.  
4. Spread out the almonds, hazelnuts, walnuts, and poppy seeds on a large bake sheet. Place in the oven and broil for a few minutes, until they have developed some color.   
5. Once they have cooled a little, place the nuts into a food processor, and blend together into a powder.  
6. Place the remaining 120 g of honey into a saucepan, bring to a boil, and simmer for 6 minutes. Remove from the heat, add the nut mixture, and stir together to combine.  
7. Using a greased rolling pin, roll the nut mixture into an even layer the same size as the sesame seed sheets. Place the layer of nuts on  one of the sesame layers, then carefully place the other sesame layer on top.  
8. Leave the cake to cool for an hour, the cut into serving pieces.
