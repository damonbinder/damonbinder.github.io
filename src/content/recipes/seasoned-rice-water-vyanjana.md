**Ingredients**

* 500 ml water from the second washing of rice, or 450 ml water plus 1 tsp rice flour  
* 20 g tamarind concentrate  
* 75 ml buttermilk  
* 15 g sugar  
* ¼ tsp ground cardamom  
* 15 ml ginger juice  
* Pinch of asafoetida  
* ½ tsp salt (modern balancing addition)

**Method**

1. Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and whisk until smooth.  
2. Heat gently until steaming and slightly thickened, about 5 minutes; do not boil hard.  
3. Taste for a balanced sweet-sour flavour and serve warm or cool.

**Notes**

* Use clean rice-washing water collected after an initial rinse that removes dust.  
* The source does not explicitly add salt, but a small amount makes the condiment more coherent.
