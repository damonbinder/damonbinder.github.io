**Ingredients**

* 1 whole dressed rabbit, about 1.2 kg, cut into 8 pieces  
* 40 g tamarind concentrate  
* 300 ml water  
* 1½ tsp salt  
* ½ tsp ground black pepper  
* ½ tsp ground dried ginger  
* 1 tsp ground cumin  
* 20 g ghee or oil

**Method**

1. Simmer the rabbit pieces with tamarind, water, and half the salt for 30–40 minutes, until nearly tender.  
2. Drain and pat dry. Thread manageable pieces onto skewers or arrange on a rack.  
3. Brush with ghee and roast at 220°C or over charcoal until browned and dry at the edges.  
4. Mix the pepper, dried ginger, cumin, and remaining salt and scatter over the hot meat.

**Notes**

* This is an explicit substitution, not a secure zoological identification.  
* Do not use wild rodents of unknown health status.
