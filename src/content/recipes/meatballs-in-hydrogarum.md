I have not, unfortunately, been able to find the pheasant fat called for in *Apicius* 2.2.1. Nevertheless, as the only recipe given for meat, rather than seafood, *isicia*, I have combined it with the recipe for *hydrogarum* that immediately follows. 

## Ingredients
*For meatballs*

* 1 kg pork mince  
* 30 ml fish sauce  
* 30 ml *defrutum*  
* ½ tsp ground black pepper

*For hydrogarum*

* 45 ml fish sauce  
* 1½ tsp dried Spanish camomile  
* 1 tsp dried lovage  
* Generous ground pepper

## Method

1. Mix the meatball ingredients together, then form into small balls.  
2. Place the *hydrogarum* ingredients into a large saucepan along with 800 ml of water. Bring to a simmer, then add the meatballs. Poach for around 10 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through, then serve the meatballs with the *hydrogarum*.
