**Ingredients**

* 6 egg whites  
* 200 g white fish fillets or chicken  
* A few sprigs of coriander leaf  
* A pinch of dried dill leaf  
* 120 ml \+ 1 Tb white wine  
* 1 Tb fish sauce  
* 1 Tb honey  
* Black pepper  
* Salt

**Method**

1. In a wide dutch oven, poach the fish or chicken in 120 ml water, along with the coriander, dill, and a couple pinches of salt. Once the meat is cooked, remove from liquid and finely chop.  
2. Remove the sprigs of coriander from the cooking liquor, and then add 120 ml white wine, and the fish sauce to the pot. Pepper to taste, and add more salt if needed.  
3. Add a pinch of salt to the egg whites, and then beat them to soft peaks. Carefully fold the minced meat in with the egg whites, then spoon the mixture onto the dutch oven so that it forms a mound floating over the liquid.  
4. Bring to a gentle simmer, then place the lid on and cook for about 10 minutes, or until the egg whites are set.   
5. Mix together the honey with the remaining tablespoon of wine. Drizzle over the set egg white foam, and serve the dish along with crusty bread.
