## Ingredients

* 500 g cooked skinless roast pork belly, chilled
* 200 g yoghurt
* 20 g sugar
* ¼ tsp ground cardamom
* Optional speck of edible camphor

## Method

1. Slice the chilled pork belly into pieces about 3 mm thick and 5–7 cm long.
2. Whisk the yoghurt with the sugar, cardamom, and camphor (if using). Fold in the pork and rest for 20 minutes before serving.

## Notes

* Use only food-grade camphor, and omit it unless you are familiar with its intensity.
