## Ingredients

* 250 g mushrooms, sliced  
* 80 ml *caroenum*  
* A small bundle of coriander leaves  
* Salt or fish sauce

## Method
Place the mushrooms in a frying pan along with the *caroenum* and the coriander leaves, and cook, stirring frequently, until the mushrooms are cooked and the sauce fully reduced. Add salt or fish sauce to taste, then serve.
