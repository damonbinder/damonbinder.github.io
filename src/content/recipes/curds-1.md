---
title: Curds
source: Apicius 7.11.9b
originalLang: Latin
original: >-

translation: >-
  Curds: with pepper and fish sauce, or salt, oil, and coriander.
category: Dips and Relishes
order: 6
---

**Ingredients**

* 150 g cottage cheese  
* 1 tsp ground coriander leaf  
* 2 tsp olive oil  
* Pinch of salt.

**Method**  
Mix everything together and serve.
