## Ingredients

* 800 g firm white fish fillets or steaks  
* 20 ml oil, for rubbing  
* 2 tsp salt, divided  
* ½ tsp turmeric  
* 30 g tamarind concentrate  
* 150 ml water  
* 60 g wheat flour  
* 700 ml neutral oil, for frying  
* ¼ tsp ground cardamom  
* ½ tsp ground black pepper

## Method

1. Rub the fish with the 20 ml oil and 1 tsp salt for 1–2 minutes. Rinse briefly in water mixed with turmeric, then pat completely dry.  
2. Dilute the tamarind in 150 ml water. Simmer the fish in this liquid for 2–4 minutes, just until the exterior firms, then drain and cool.  
3. Dust every surface with wheat flour.  
4. Fry at 180°C until browned and cooked through.  
5. Mix the cardamom, pepper, and remaining salt and sprinkle over the hot fish.

**Notes**

* The initial rubbing is a cleaning and deodorizing technique rather than a marinade.  
* Cardamom on fried fish is explicit and gives the dish a distinctive finish.
