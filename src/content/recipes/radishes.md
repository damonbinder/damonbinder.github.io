source: Apicius 3.14
work: Apicius
originalLang: Latin
original: >-
  XIIII. RAFANOS. rafanos cum piperato ita ut piper cum liquamine teras.
translation: >-
  Radishes. Radishes with a peppered dressing, prepared by grinding pepper
  with fish sauce.
category: Vegetables
subcategory: Salads
order: 112
---

**Ingredients**

* 5 medium radishes  
* 1 tsp fish sauce  
* ½ tsp black peppercorns

**Method**  
Slice the radishes. Ground the pepper corns with the fish sauce in a mortar and pestle, drizzle over the radishes, and serve.
