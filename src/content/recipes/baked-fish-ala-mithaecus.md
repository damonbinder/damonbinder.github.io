subcategory: Greek Fish

---

**Ingredients**

* 500g red bandfish fillets (or other soft white fish, such as whiting or sole)  
* 120g feta cheese  
* 60 ml olive oil

**Method**  
Using a mortar and pestle, grind the cheese and olive oil together into a paste. Place the fish fillets on a baking sheet, cover them with the cheese mixture, and broil for 10 minutes or until cooked through.
