---
title: Roast Lamb with a Coriander Crust
source: Apicius 8.6.8
category: Meat
subcategory: Lamb and Goat
order: 50
---

**Ingredients**

* 2 kg leg of lamb  
* 25 g whole coriander seeds  
* 2 tsp ground black pepper  
* 2 tsp salt  
* 2 Tb olive oil

**Method**

1. Preheat the oven to 180C/350F  
2. In a mortar and pestle, crush the coriander seeds into a coarse mixture, then mix with the salt and black pepper. Combine with the olive oil and rub all over the leg of lamb.  
3. Roast the lamb in the oven until rare, around 80 minutes, or until a meat thermometer reads 60C/140F in the thickest part of the lamb.  
4. Remove the lamb from the oven. Loosely cover in foil and allow to rest for 10 minutes, before carving. Serve with an Apician sauce of your choice.
