---
title: Conditum
source: Konditon, Orisbasius 5.33i
category: Beverages
order: 185
---

**Ingredients**

* 300 ml dry white wine  
* 140 g honey  
* A pinch of ground pepper

**Method**  
Mix the wine, honey, and pepper together. Add water as desired; a wine-to-water ratio between 1:2 and 2:3 was common.
