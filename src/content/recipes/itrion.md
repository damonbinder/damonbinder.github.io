**Ingredients**

* 200g sesame seeds  
* 180g honey

**Method**

1. Line a large tray with parchment paper  
2. Place the sesame seeds in a large frying pan and toast until aromatic and golden, reduce the heat to keep them warm.  
3. Place the honey into a saucepan. Heat until it reaches 125C/260F, then immediately pour in the toasted sesame seeds. Reduce the heat and cook for a further few minutes, stirring constantly.  
4. Pour the mixture out onto the lined tray and spread evenly into a thin layer. Allow to cool completely, then cut however you like and serve.
