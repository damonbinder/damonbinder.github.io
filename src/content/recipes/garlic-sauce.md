## Ingredients

* ½ tsp ground pepper  
* 1 tsp ground cumin   
* 1 tsp ground coriander  
* ½ tsp dried oregano  
* 2 cloves garlic, minced  
* 30 ml fish sauce  
* 30 ml olive oil  
* 45 ml grape syrup  
* 180 ml wine

## Method
Place all the ingredients into a small saucepan. Gentle bring to a simmer and cook for a few minutes, then serve with meat or fish.
