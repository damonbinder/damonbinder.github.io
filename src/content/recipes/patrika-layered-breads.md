**Ingredients**

* 300 g fine wheat flour  
* ¾ tsp salt  
* 180 ml water, approximately  
* 50 g ghee, melted  
* Rice flour, for dusting

**Method**

1. Mix the flour and salt, add enough water for a soft dough, knead until smooth, and rest for 30 minutes.  
2. Divide into 10 balls and roll each as thinly as possible.  
3. Brush a griddle lightly with ghee and cook each sheet for about 30 seconds per side, keeping it pale.  
4. Brush each cooked sheet with a trace of ghee and stack them immediately.  
5. Cut the stack into wedges or serve whole.

**Notes**

* The source does not state whether the sheets were fused into one cake or simply stacked for service.  
* Using sifted flour reflects the instruction to remove coarse particles.
