## Ingredients

* 1 kg split beef, lamb, or goat marrow bones  
* 20 ml lemon juice or tamarind water  
* ¼ tsp asafoetida  
* ½ tsp ground black pepper  
* ½ tsp ground cumin or caraway  
* ¾ tsp rock salt

## Method

1. Roast the bones cut-side up at 230°C for 15–20 minutes, until the marrow is soft and bubbling.  
2. Scoop the marrow into a small pan.  
3. Add the lemon juice, asafoetida, pepper, cumin or caraway, and salt.  
4. Cook over low heat for 2–3 minutes, stirring gently, and serve at once with flatbread.

**Notes**

* Using pre-split bones replaces the source’s method of cracking whole roasted bones with stones.
