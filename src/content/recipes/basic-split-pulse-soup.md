  Use rāja-mudga, yellow mung, niṣpāva, chickpeas, dark āḍhakī, black gram, lentils, or rāja-māṣa. Cook them split or whole according to taste. ## Ingredients

* 250 g split mung, red lentils, split chickpeas, or urad dal  
* 900 ml water, plus more as needed  
* ¼ tsp asafoetida  
* ½ tsp ground turmeric  
* 1 tsp fine rock salt, plus more to taste

## Method

1. Rinse the pulse until the water runs mostly clear.  
2. Combine the pulse, water, asafoetida, and turmeric in a saucepan. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a very gentle simmer.  
3. Cook partly covered until soft, adding small amounts of hot water whenever the mixture becomes too thick: about 20 minutes for red lentils, 30–40 minutes for split mung, or 45–60 minutes for split chickpeas.  
4. Stir in the salt, and serve.
