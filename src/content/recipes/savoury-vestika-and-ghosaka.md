## Ingredients

* 200 g split chickpeas, urad dal, or yellow split peas  
* 15 g fresh ginger, grated  
* 1 tsp ground cumin  
* ¼ tsp asafoetida  
* 1 tsp rock salt  
* 300 g maṇḍaka dough  
* 20 g ghee or oil, for the griddle

## Method

1. Cook the pulse until tender, drain very well, and mash coarsely.  
2. Mix with ginger, cumin, asafoetida, and salt. Cool completely.  
3. Divide the dough and filling into 8 portions. Enclose the filling and roll each into a 12–15 cm round.  
4. Cook on a medium-hot griddle brushed with ghee, 2–3 minutes per side, until browned in spots and cooked through.

**Notes**

* *Harimantha* is uncertain; mung or another small pulse is a reasonable choice.  
* The source differentiates veṣṭikā and ghosaka, but the practical reconstructions overlap substantially.
