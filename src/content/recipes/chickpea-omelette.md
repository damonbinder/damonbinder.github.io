---
title: Chickpea omelette
source: Apicius 5.8.2b
category: Legumes
subcategory: Other Legumes
order: 158
---

**Ingredients**

* 400g can chickpeas, drained  
* 6 eggs  
* 1 tsp chopped green fennel  
* 1½  fish sauce  
* 3 Tb *defrutum*  
* Pepper  
* Olive oil

**Method**

1. Place the chickpeas in a frying pan along with a little olive oil and heat gently.  
2. Whisk together the eggs, then pour over the chickpeas and cook, flipping if needed.  
3. In a small bowl, mix together the fennel, fish sauce, *defrutum*, and a grind of black pepper. Once the omelette is cooked, plate and serve with the sauce drizzled over.
