**Ingredients**

* 1 kg very fresh firm fish, cut into 8–10 cm pieces  
* 80 g non-iodized salt  
* Optional: 1 tsp turmeric

**Method**

1. Coat the fish thoroughly with the salt and optional turmeric.  
2. Pack into a non-reactive container, cover, and refrigerate for 24–48 hours.  
3. Rinse off excess salt and soak in cold water for 30–60 minutes, changing the water once.  
4. Dry thoroughly, then grill or roast at 220°C until browned and fully cooked.

**Notes**

* This is not a validated room-temperature preservation method. Keep the fish refrigerated throughout.  
* The original jars may have held a much more heavily salted, longer-lasting product.
