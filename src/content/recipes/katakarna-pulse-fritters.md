**Ingredients**

* 100 g yellow split peas  
* 100 g split chickpeas  
* 50 g fava-bean flour or chickpea flour  
* 150–200 ml water  
* 15 g ghee, melted  
* 1 tsp rock salt  
* ¼ tsp asafoetida  
* ¼ tsp instant yeast or 2 Tb sourdough starter  
* 700 ml neutral oil, for frying

**Method**

1. Soak the split peas and chickpeas overnight. Drain and grind with just enough water to make a thick, slightly coarse batter.  
2. Beat in the fava-bean flour, ghee, salt, asafoetida, and yeast or starter.  
3. Cover and leave at room temperature for 2–4 hours, until faintly aerated.  
4. Spread portions into rough discs about 1 cm thick on a wet plate or oiled sheet.  
5. Fry at 175°C until puffed, blistered, and golden, turning once.

**Notes**

* *Kaṇḍu* is uncertain and may not be a ferment; fermentation is a practical interpretation rather than a secure translation.  
* Niṣpāva flour is represented here by fava-bean flour.
