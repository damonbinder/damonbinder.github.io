**Ingredients**

* 500 g lamb liver, cut into 4 cm pieces  
* 250 g lamb fat or pork back fat, cut into similar pieces  
* 1½ tsp rock salt  
* 1 tsp ground black pepper

**Method**

1. Alternate liver and fat on metal skewers.  
2. Grill over medium-hot charcoal, turning frequently, until the fat is browned and the liver is just cooked through.  
3. Sprinkle generously with salt and pepper and serve immediately.

**Notes**

* The reconstruction treats kālakhaṇḍa as liver, but the organ identification is uncertain.  
* Do not overcook liver; the fat should render enough to baste it.
