**Ingredients**

* 150 g coarse semolina or cracked wheat  
* 250 ml whole milk  
* 60 g ghee  
* 75 g sugar  
* ½ tsp ground cardamom  
* ¼ tsp finely ground black pepper  
* Pinch of salt

**Method**

1. Soak the semolina or cracked wheat in the milk for 20 minutes.  
2. Melt the ghee in a heavy pan. Add the soaked grain and cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, until thick and lightly toasted.  
3. Add the sugar, cardamom, pepper, and salt. Continue stirring until the mixture pulls away from the pan, 5–8 minutes.  
4. Serve warm in small portions.

**Notes**

* The black pepper is explicit and should remain perceptible, though not dominant.  
* If using cracked wheat rather than semolina, simmer it in the milk until tender before frying in ghee.
