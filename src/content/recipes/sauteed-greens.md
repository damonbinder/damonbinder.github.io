---
title: Sauteed Greens
source: Apicius 3.16b

  liquamine oleo aceto a manu uel in patina piper cumino bacis lentisci.
translation: >-
  XVI. WILD HERBS. With fish sauce, oil, and vinegar, either by hand [i.e.
  dressed raw] or in a *patina*, with pepper, cumin, and mastic berries.
category: Vegetables
subcategory: Unclassified
order: 133
---

**Ingredients**

* 500 g fresh leafy vegetables   
  (eg spinach, chard, mustard greens)  
* 1 Tb fish sauce  
* 1 Tb olive oil  
* 2 tsp white wine vinegar  
* Ground pepper  
* 1 tsp cumin  
* 10 small grains of mastic gum

**Method**

1. Wash and chop the leafy vegetables as desired  
2. Crush the mastic gum in a mortar and pestle. Place all of the ingredients into a frying pan and saute the greens until cooked, adding a little water if desired.
