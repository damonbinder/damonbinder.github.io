## Ingredients

* 1 L whole milk  
* 40 ml lemon juice  
* 80 g sugar  
* ½ tsp ground cardamom  
* 30 g ripe tamarind pulp, lightly toasted in a dry pan  
* 300 ml strained fruit juice, such as pomegranate, mango, grape, or lime  
* Cold water as needed

## Method

1. Bring the milk to a boil, lower the heat, and add the lemon juice until it separates.  
2. Pour through a cloth and collect the whey. Stir the sugar and cardamom into the warm whey, then filter again until relatively clear.  
3. Soak the toasted tamarind in 100 ml warm whey, strain, and add enough to give a pale amber colour and mild sourness.  
4. Cool completely, mix with the fruit juice, and dilute with cold water to taste.  
5. Serve chilled.

**Notes**

* The exact cloth-and-pressing procedure is compressed and may involve clarifying the curdled milk differently.  
* The curds left from the process can be used in the kṣīra-prakāra recipe.
