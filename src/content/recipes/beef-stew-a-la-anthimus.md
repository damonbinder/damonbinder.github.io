**Ingredients**

* 1 kg beef stew meat, chopped into bite-sized cubes  
* 120 ml red wine vinegar  
* 2 small leeks, chopped  
* 500 g celeriac, peeled and cubed  
* ½ tsp dried pennyroyal  
* 120 ml honey  
* 1 tsp ground black black pepper  
* ⅓  tsp ground costus  
* ⅓ tsp ground spikenard  
* ¼ tsp ground clove  
* 2 Tb white wine  
* Salt to taste

**Method**

1. Place the beef in a dutch oven, and add water so that the beef is just covered. Bring to a gentle boil, and then skim off any foam.  
2. Add the vinegar, leeks, celeriac, and pennyroyal, then simmer with the lid on for 1 hour.  
3. Add the honey to the saucepan, and salt to taste. Adjust the sweet/sour balance if desired.  
4. Mix together the ground spices along the wine to disperse. Pour into the stew, and then simmer for a further 10 minutes. Remove from the heat and serve.
