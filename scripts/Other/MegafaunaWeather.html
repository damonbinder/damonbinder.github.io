Megafauna Extinction Simulator 2016

By Damon Binder

Each square represents about an 8km by 8km area.

The total area is about 640 000 square kilometers.

The colour represents the number of megafauna, which in ideal conditions are around 20 per square.

Each human group represents a band of about 30 people.

From population estimates an area this size could support about 25000 people, or about 800 groups.

You need a modern browser which supports HTML5 canvas to view this.
