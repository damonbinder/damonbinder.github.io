## Ingredients

* 500 g unsalted cultured butter  
* 1 whole wheat grain  
* 1 fresh edible betel leaf, washed and dried

## Method

1. Melt the butter in a heavy saucepan over low heat.  
2. Continue cooking gently until the water has evaporated, the milk solids are golden, and the fat is clear.  
3. Add the wheat grain and betel leaf, remove from the heat, and leave for 2 minutes.  
4. Strain through fine cloth into a dry jar.

**Notes**

* The preliminary washing of butter is unnecessary for most modern butter but reflects removal of residual buttermilk.  
* Use culinary betel leaf from a reliable source, not ornamental plants.
