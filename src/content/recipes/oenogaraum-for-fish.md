source: Apicius 10.2.17/18
originalLang: Latin
original: >-
  in pisce oenogarum: teres piper rutam, mel commissces, passum liquamen
  caroenum et sic igni mollissimo calefacies. in pisce oenogarum: ut supra
  facies. cum bullierit amulo obligabis.
translation: >-
  *Oenogarum* for fish: grind pepper and rue, mix in honey, [add] *passum*,
  fish sauce, and *caroenum*, and heat it thus over a very gentle fire.
  *Oenogarum* for fish: prepare as above. When it has bubbled up, bind it with
  starch.
category: Seafood
subcategory: Apician Sauces for Fish
order: 91
---

**Ingredients**

* 2 Tb *passum*  
* 1 Tb *defrutum*  
* 1 Tb honey  
* 1 Tb fish sauce  
* ½ tsp dried rue  
* Ground pepper

**Method**  
Place the ingredient into a small saucepan, mix together, and gently bring to heat. Thicken with a little cornstarch if desired.
