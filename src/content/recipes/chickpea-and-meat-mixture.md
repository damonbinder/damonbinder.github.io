## Ingredients

* 250 g cooked chickpeas, drained  
* 250 g boneless lamb or pork, cut into 2 cm pieces  
* 100 g green chickpeas or edamame  
* 100 g cooked fava beans  
* 1 small onion, cut into pieces  
* 2 garlic cloves, sliced  
* 20 g fresh ginger, cut into small pieces  
* ½ tsp turmeric  
* 1 tsp ground cumin  
* 1 tsp ground coriander  
* ½ tsp ground dried ginger  
* ¼ tsp asafoetida  
* 30 ml oil  
* 200 ml sour meat glaze  
* 1 tsp salt

## Method

1. Mash half the cooked chickpeas and form them into small rough nuggets; leave the rest whole.  
2. Heat the oil and brown the meat. Add the onion, garlic, and fresh ginger and cook for 3 minutes.  
3. Add the chickpea nuggets, whole chickpeas, green chickpeas, fava beans, dry spices, and salt.  
4. Pour in the sour glaze and cook uncovered over medium heat until the meat is tender and nearly all the liquid has reduced.

**Notes**

* *Kola* is uncertain and omitted; jujube is possible but does not fit the context securely.  
* The shaped chickpea pieces are reconstructed; the text may instead refer simply to equal pieces of cooked chickpeas or chickpea paste.
