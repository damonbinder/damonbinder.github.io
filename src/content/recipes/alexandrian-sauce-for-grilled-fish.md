---
title: Alexandrian Sauce for Grilled Fish
source: Apicius 10.1.7
category: Seafood
subcategory: Apician Sauces for Fish
order: 89
---

**Ingredients**

* Pepper  
* Pinch of lovage  
* 1 tsp chopped coriander leaf  
* 30g raisins, finely chopped  
* 50 ml white wine  
* 50 ml *passum*  
* 1 Tb fish sauce  
* 1 Tb olive oil

**Method**  
Mix everything into a small saucepan. Bring to a simmer and cook for a few minutes, then serve with the fish.

**Notes**

* Makes enough for 500 g of fish
