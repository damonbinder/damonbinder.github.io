**Ingredients**

* 1 L whole milk  
* 30 g plain live-culture yoghurt or cultured buttermilk  
* Optional: 20 g sugar  
* Optional: ¼ tsp ground cardamom

**Method**

1. Reduce the milk gently to 500 ml, stirring often.  
2. Cool to 42–45°C. Whisk in the yoghurt or buttermilk and the optional sugar or cardamom.  
3. Pour into a clean container, cover, and incubate undisturbed at about 40°C for 6–10 hours, until set and pleasantly tart.  
4. Refrigerate immediately.

**Notes**

* Six yāmas would be far longer than needed under warm modern incubation and may reflect a different time reckoning or ambient temperature.  
* Use live-culture dairy as the starter; boiling after inoculation will prevent setting.
