translation: >-
  Sauce for all game, boiled or roasted: eight *scripuli* of pepper; six
  *scripuli* each of rue, lovage, celery seed, juniper, thyme, and dried mint;
  three *scripuli* of pennyroyal. Reduce all this to a very fine powder,
  combine it with wine, and grind. Add into a small vessel as much honey as is
  enough, and use this with *oxygarum*.

**Ingredients**

* 12 peppercorns  
* 2 pinches dried rue  
* 2 pinches dried lovage  
* 2 pinches celery seed  
* 2 juniper berries  
* 2 pinches dried thyme  
* 2 small pinches mint  
* 1 small pinch pennyroyal  
* 1 tsp wine  
* 2 Tb honey  
* 1 Tb fish sauce  
* 1 Tb red wine vinegar

**Method**  
Using a mortar and pestle, grind the herbs and spices together to a fine powder. Add the wine, then grind again. Combine with the honey, fish sauce, and vinegar. Serve with game, or with roast beef or lamb. 

**Notes**

* This is similar to the *oxyporium* recipes, basically calls for making a spiced honey which is then used with *oxygarum*
