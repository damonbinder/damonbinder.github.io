**Ingredients**

* 250g split peas  
* 30 ml white wine vinegar  
* 30 ml olive oil, plus additional for serving  
* 2 hard boiled eggs  
* ¼ onion, finely diced  
* Salt

**Method**

1. Place peas in a saucepan along with 750 ml of water. Bring to a simmer, then cover and cook for about 1 hour, or until the peas are mushy and smooth.   
2. Remove the peas from the heat and allow to cool for a little, before placing in the fridge to chill completely.  
3. When ready to finish the meal, separate the hard boiled egg whites and yokes. Mince the egg whites and add to the peas along with the vinegar, olive oil, and onion. Salt to taste.  
4. Place the pea mixture into a serving dish. Garnish by pushing the egg yolk through a strainer over the dish, then drizzle on a little more olive oil, and serve.
