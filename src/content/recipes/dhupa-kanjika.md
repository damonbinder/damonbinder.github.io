**Ingredients**

* 500 ml strained rice kāñjika or another mild sour fermented grain liquid  
* ¾ tsp rock salt  
* ½ tsp roasted ground cumin  
* Pinch of asafoetida

**Method**

1. Whisk the kāñjika with the salt, cumin, and asafoetida.  
2. Chill or serve at room temperature.

**Notes**

* *Sauvīra* and *kāñjika* overlap as sour fermented grain liquids in many sources; the exact distinction here is uncertain.  
* Keep homemade fermented grain liquids refrigerated after they reach the desired acidity.
