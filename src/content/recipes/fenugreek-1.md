title: Fenugreek
source: Apicius 5.7, see also Galen
category: Legumes
subcategory: Other Legumes
order: 156
---

**Ingredients**

* 3 Tb fenugreek seeds  
* 1 Tb olive oil  
* 2 tsp white wine  
* 1 tsp fish sauce

**Method**  
Soak the fenugreek seeds in a cup of water overnight in the fridge. Drain, then mix with the olive oil, wine, and fish sauce. Serve with bread.
