title: Roast Lamb with Date Sauce

  IIII, piperis unciam I, salis modicum laseris modicum. ius [in] ipsius:
  oleum acetabulum liquaminis acetabulum mellis acetabulum dactilos tritos

translation: >-
  Another way, kid or lamb *syringiatus* [injected/piped with the stuffing]:
  one *sextarius* of milk, four ounces of honey, one ounce of pepper, a little
  salt, a little *laser*. Its own sauce: a cup of oil, a cup of fish sauce, a
  cup of honey, eight ground dates, a *hemina* of good wine, a little starch.
category: Meat
subcategory: Lamb and Goat

---

**Ingredients**

* 1.5 kg leg of lamb  
* 270 ml milk  
* 1 tsp black peppercorns, crushed  
* Pinch of asafoetida  
* 80 g honey  
* 60 ml fish sauce  
* 30 ml olive oil  
* 140 ml white wine  
* 4 dates, softened in boiling water and pounded into a paste  
* 1 Tb cornstarch, made into slurry

**Method**

1. Preheat the oven to 180C/475F.   
2. Place the lamb into a dutch oven along with 30 ml of fish sauce and 300 ml of water. Cook in the oven, covered, for 80 mins, then remove the lid and continue cooking until desired doneness is reached. Remove from the oven and allow to rest.  
3. Place all of the sauce ingredients, except for the cornstarch slurry, into a saucepan, along with a few tablespoons of the reserved cooking liquor. Taste and adjust seasoning as desired. Gently heat until it just begins to simmer, then thicken with the cornstarch.  
4. Carve the lamb into thick slices, pour over the sauce and serve.
