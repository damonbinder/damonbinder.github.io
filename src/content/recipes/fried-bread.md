---
title: Fried Bread
source: Apicius 7.11.3
category: Dessert
subcategory: Pancakes and Fritters
order: 171
---

**Ingredients**

* A loaf of bread, sliced and with the crusts removed  
* Milk  
* Olive oil  
* Honey

**Method**  
Soak the bread in milk, then fry in olive oil until golden brown. Pour honey over and serve.
