## Ingredients

* A large bundle of chard leaves  
* 1 Tb olive oil  
* 1 tsp Djon mustard   
* 1 tsp vinegar  
* Salt

## Method

1. Clean and boil the chard until cooked, then remove and strain in a colander.  
2. Mix the remaining ingredients in a bowl, then toss with the chard and serve.
