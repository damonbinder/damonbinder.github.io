**Ingredients**

* 500 g cleaned calf or lamb brains  
* 500 ml lightly sour rice kāñjika, or 450 ml water plus 50 ml rice vinegar  
* 1 tsp salt  
* 30 g ghee or oil  
* ½ tsp ground cumin  
* ½ tsp ground black pepper  
* ¼ tsp asafoetida  
* ¼ tsp turmeric

**Method**

1. Soak the brains in cold salted water for 30 minutes and remove any visible membrane.  
2. Poach gently in the kāñjika for 8–10 minutes, then lift out and cool.  
3. Cut into large pieces. Heat the ghee with cumin, pepper, asafoetida, and turmeric.  
4. Add the brain and fry carefully until lightly browned on the surfaces.

**Notes**

* Only use inspected commercially prepared brains.  
* Brain is delicate; keep the poach below a hard boil and turn the pieces gently.
