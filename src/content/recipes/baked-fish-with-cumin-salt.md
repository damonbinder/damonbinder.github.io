## Ingredients

* 800 g porgy, scaled and gutted (as a substitute for Mediterranean parrotfish)  
* 150 g feta cheese  
* 75 ml olive oil, plus additional for serving  
* 1 tsp salt  
* 1½ tsp cumin seeds

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 220 C/ 430 F  
2. Using a mortar and pestle, grind the cheese and olive oil together into a paste.  
3. Baste the fish on one side with some of the cheese-oil mixture. Then place that side on a wire rack over a baking tray, and baste the other side of the fish, placing some of the mixture also in the gut cavity. Place the fish in the oven and bake for 15–25 minutes, until it reaches 60C in the thickest part of the fish, then remove.  
4. While the fish roasts, grind the cumin and salt together into a fine powder. Serve the fish alongside the cumin salt and with additional olive oil, if desired. s
