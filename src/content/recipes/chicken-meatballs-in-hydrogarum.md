*Apicius* does not tell us how to make chicken *isicia*, but other recipes for *isicia* generally call for fish sauce, pepper, and other flavorings. If you like, add 1–2 Tb of fish sauce to the chicken mince, along with ½ tsp ground black pepper and a pinch of asafoetida.

## Ingredients

* 800 g chicken mince
* 1320 ml water  
* 100 ml *caroenum*  
* 100 ml fish sauce  
* 30 peppercorns, crushed

## Method

1. Add the water, caroenum, fish sauce, and pepper to a large pan and heat to just below a simmer.  
2. Roll the chicken mince to meatballs and add to the large pan. Poach until the meatballs are cooked through, then serve.
