**Ingredients**

* 1 tsp white pepper  
* ½ tsp celery seed  
* ½ tsp laser root  
* 1 tsp ground parmesan  
* 1 Tb honey  
* 1 Tb fish sauce  
* 1 Tb vinegar

**Method**  
Mix all the ingredients together. Serve with lettuce as a salad dressing, or consume alongside lettuce by the teaspoon.
