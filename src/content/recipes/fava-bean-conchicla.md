**Ingredients**

* 800 g frozen fava beans  
* 100 ml white wine  
* 1 tsp lovage  
* 1 tsp cumin  
* 1 tsp pepper  
* Handful chopped coriander leaf  
* 2 Tb fish sauce  
* 2 Tb olive oil

**Method**

1. Thaw the fava beans either by leaving them in the fridge for a few hours, or by running them under cold water. Remove the fava bean skins and discard.  
2. Place the fava beans in a pot with enough water to cover. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook until very soft, around 15 to 20 minutes.  
3. Drain the fava beans and place back into the saucepan, along with the remaining ingredients. Mash the fava beans into a smooth, thick mash, and then bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Cook for a final few minutes and then serve.
