## Ingredients

* 2 L whole milk, preferably buffalo milk  
* Optional: 50–100 g sugar, depending on intended use  
* Optional: ½ tsp cardamom

## Method

1. Pour the milk into a wide heavy saucepan and bring to a gentle simmer.  
2. Cook over the lowest practical heat, stirring and scraping the base and sides frequently.  
3. For drinkable reduced milk, stop at 1 L. For spoonable milk, stop at about 650 ml. For a dense mass, continue to about 330 ml. For a fudge-like confection, reduce towards 250 ml.  
4. Add sugar or cardamom only near the end if desired.

**Notes**

* Reduction to one-eighth is liable to scorch and may be better understood as a named confection rather than a precise modern ratio.  
* Weighing the pot before and during cooking is the easiest way to track reduction accurately.
