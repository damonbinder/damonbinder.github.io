## Ingredients

* 500 g chicken mince  
* 350 g cabbage, finely chopped  
* 150 g leek, finely chopped  
* 1 tsp ground black pepper  
* 1 tsp lovage  
* 180 ml wine  
* 90 ml fish sauce  
* 90 ml honey  
* 30 ml olive oil  
* 120 ml milk  
* 3 eggs

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 160C/300F  
2. Form the chicken mince into small meatballs. Place in a pot of simmering water and cook until cooked through, then drain.  
3. In a separate saucepan, boil the cabbage and leek for 5 minutes, then drain.  
4. In a large casserole dish, add half the cabbages and leek. Next form a layer of meatballs, followed by a second layer of cabbages and leek, and finish with a second layer of meatballs.  
5. Mix together the pepper, lovage, wine, fish sauce, honey, and oil, then pour over the casserole. Place in the oven for 20 minutes.  
6. Whisk together the milk and the eggs. Gently pour over the top of the casserole, and then put it back to bake for another 20 minutes, or until set on top. Remove from the oven and serve.
