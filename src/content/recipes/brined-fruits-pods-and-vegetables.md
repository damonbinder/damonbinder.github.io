**Ingredients**

* 1 kg mixed firm produce: green mango, small eggplant, lime, gooseberry, cucumber, ridge gourd, grapes, green beans, or similar  
* 1 L water  
* 60 g non-iodized salt  
* Optional: 1 tsp mustard seed and ½ tsp fenugreek seed

**Method**

1. Wash the produce and leave small items whole; cut larger items into 3–4 cm pieces.  
2. Dissolve the salt in the water by boiling, then cool completely.  
3. Pack the produce into a clean jar and cover fully with brine, using a weight to keep it submerged.  
4. Ferment at cool room temperature for 2–5 days, tasting daily, then refrigerate.

**Notes**

* The source may describe preservation in plain strong brine rather than active fermentation.  
* Discard the batch if it develops mould, putrid odour, or unusual colours. Keep all produce submerged.
