**Ingredients**

* 100 ml cream  
* 2 tsp honey  
* Pinch of cinnamon  
* Small pinch of spikenard

**Method**  
Mix together and serve alongside seasonally appropriate fruits—pears, pomegranates, dates, raisins—or nuts such as almonds, pistachios, and pine nuts.
