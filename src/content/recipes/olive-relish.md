**Ingredients**

* 120 g black olives  
* 120 g green olives  
* 40 ml red wine vinegar  
* 60 ml olive oil  
* ½ tsp cumin  
* 1 tsp coriander seed  
* 1 tsp ground fennel seed  
* 2 tsp dried rue  
* 2 tsp dried mint

**Method**  
Chop the olives, then add the remaining ingredients. Place in a sealable container and pour a little more olive oil over to cover.
